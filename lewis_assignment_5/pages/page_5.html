Runs Great

Last on this topic from our point of view, but certainly not least, is that they need to run great. Can you imagine seeing a beautiful classic car being towed to a car show, not for reasons of just trying to keep it clean and off the road, but because it will not start, or doesn't run good. It would be the laughingstock of the car show. Everybody wants to hear that beautiful deep tone coming from the engine, and exhaust. The same goes for your beautiful website. If you have a visually beautiful website, but let's say that a button does not work that is supposed to bring you to another page on that site, well users will likely leave your page thinking about how poorly it is running. Another big thing you want to think about with your website is how well it loads. Users will not wait around for your page to load if it is taking forever. They will certainly move on to the next page. Now after learning some of the similarities, what do you think? Are there any other similarities that you can think of?
