The WOW Factor!

One of the biggest things that people want to see in their classic car, or website, or somebody else's is that wow factor. What exactly is that wow factor? That wow factor is what will keep your eyes drawn to that classic car, or beautiful website. The wow factor in classic cars is all those custom things done to it. It could be that beautiful, shiny custom paint job, those clean, shiny wheels and tires, custom, or restored interiors, or the custom work done under the hood. Just like with classic cars, websites will have that wow factor with custom things done to them, that makes them stand out from the rest. Some things that can give your website that wow factor are great transitions, beautiful color schemes, good interactivity, or even just a really nice font that goes great with your site.
