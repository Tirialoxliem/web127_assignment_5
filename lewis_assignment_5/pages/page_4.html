Nice and Clean

A very important part of having a beautiful classic car, or beautiful website is to keep it nice, and clean. I mean, who wants to see a dirty beat up classic car, or a horrible website with no upkeep? Can you imagine going to a car show, and seeing a potentially beautiful classic car sitting there, that is dirty with "clean me" written on the back of it with someone's finger swipes in the dirt? That would be down right awful. The same rules apply to your website. You want your website to be nice and clean, and not a big, jumbled mess.
